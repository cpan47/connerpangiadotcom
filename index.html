About Me

Welcome! I'm a passionate organic vegetable farmer and web developer bridging the worlds of sustainable agriculture and technology. Since 2016, I've been dedicated to growing high-quality organic food while recently expanding into web development as a complementary career path.

My farming journey began with studying permaculture in 2015, leading me to establish Happy Now Farm in Brentwood, NY. We specialize in mixed vegetables including lettuce, tomatoes, and cucumbers, while partnering with local farms to offer over 600 products through our online farmers market platform. I'm also proud to work with Pleasant Valley Farm, one of the most technologically sophisticated farms using advanced data management systems.

Sustainable agriculture fills me with purpose and meaning. There's something deeply satisfying about the tangible, beneficial impact on both soil health and community wellbeing. When I'm not farming or coding, you can find me playing Doc Watson songs on guitar or smoking jerky on my Traeger.
